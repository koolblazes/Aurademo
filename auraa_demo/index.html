Lagos has more vehicles per square kilometre than almost anywhere in Africa — yet most sit parked while their owners commute to work, attend meetings, or run errands. Meanwhile, thousands of skilled drivers have no structured, insured, dignified way to work.

01

Your car spends 8–12 hours a day in a car park or compound while you work. It depreciates. It earns nothing. AURAA turns it into a managed income-generating asset — without you lifting a finger.

Talented drivers in Lagos have no formal employment structure, no insurance, no guaranteed income. AURAA gives them vetted access to vehicles, structured trips, and earnings they can count on.

The Platform

Three roles.
One ecosystem.

AURAA is not a ride-hailing app. It is a vehicle-centric mobility infrastructure — a three-sided platform where vehicle owners, verified drivers, and riders all win on every trip.

When a rider books an AURAA GO trip, the fare is automatically split: the driver earns 55%, the vehicle owner earns 25%, and AURAA retains 20% to cover insurance, operations, and growth. No manual settlements. No disputes. The platform handles everything.

For Organisations

The true cost of
an in-house driver
is never just the salary.

Most Lagos organisations employ one or more full-time drivers. It feels like control. The reality is a fixed overhead that pays whether the driver works or not — and a liability that most HR teams underestimate until something goes wrong.

The Traditional Model — What it actually costs

Your In-House Driver

  • Monthly salary paid regardless of trips made — holidays, sick days, downtime included
  • Accommodation, feeding allowance, and welfare obligations that compound over time
  • No insurance coverage if an accident occurs — liability falls on the organisation
  • No dashcam or trip record — disputes have no evidence trail
  • Termination is a legal and emotional process — not a button to press
  • One driver means one vehicle, one schedule, zero flexibility
  • Background checks, if done, are rarely formalised or repeated
AURAA ASSIST — What you actually get

AURAA ASSIST for Your Organisation

  • Pay only for trips made — no salary, no allowance, no idle cost
  • Zero accommodation or welfare obligation — AURAA handles driver welfare
  • Every trip insured by Heirs Insurance Group — liability is covered
  • Every trip recorded via SmartCam — full accountability and evidence
  • Need more drivers? Book more. Need fewer? Just don't book — zero friction
  • Access a pool of verified drivers — not one person whose absence stops everything
  • All drivers background-checked, ID-verified, and medically cleared before onboarding
